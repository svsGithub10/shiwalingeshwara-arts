About Us

25+ Years of Artistic Excellence

Shivalingeshwara Arts has been creating artistic works in Haveri for more than 25 years. Founded by Veeranna Sheelavantar, our studio is known for wedding decorations, thermocol designs, laser cut works, engravings and creative artworks.

Serving Many Places

Our workshop is located at Hukkeri Math Old Complex, Haveri – 581110. We deliver orders to many towns and districts using courier services and bus transport.
